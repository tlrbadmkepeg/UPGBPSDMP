Privacy Policy – UPG BPSDMP

Official application of the Ministry of Trade, managed by the Human Resources Development Agency for Trade (BPSDMP).

App Owner: Ministry of Trade – BPSDMP

1. Data Collected

  • Identity Data: name, employee ID, unit, position.
  • Contact: valid email and WhatsApp number.
  • Reporting information: details of gratuities and supporting files.
  • Technical data: device type, app version, and basic logs for reliability.

2. Purpose of Processing

  • Verification and validation of gratuity reporting.
  • Follow-up monitoring by the Gratuity Control Unit (UPG).
  • Improving service, security, and application reliability.

3. Legal Basis

Data processing is conducted in compliance with Indonesian regulations and the official duties of government institutions.

4. Storage & Security

  • Data is stored on systems designated by the Ministry of Trade.
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel with authentication control.
  • Data transmission is protected with HTTPS/TLS encryption.

5. Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or traded. Data may only be shared with authorities if required by law.

6. User Rights

  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request information on data processing.
  • Submit inquiries/objections via official contact.

7. Retention & Deletion

Data is retained as long as needed for reporting purposes and archiving obligations, with deletion or anonymization following government policy.

8. Children

This app is not intended for children under 18.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be announced via the app or this page.
